The Vision

Greenchip is the critique network for the next generation of artists. A space built on respect, depth, and growth - where artists share work, give and receive meaningful critique, and build their practice in public.

✦
Our mission is to help artists level up through honest feedback and real connection.

Our Approach

We will build an intuitive, high-quality native mobile app using Expo with a powerful web platform for account management and billing. The core experience will be fast, focused, and community-first.

Delivery Plan

A 16-week build organized around working software, reviewable milestones, and a production launch. Each phase ends with a demo and a clear acceptance checkpoint.

1Weeks 1-2

Foundation & Design Alignment

  • Technical architecture and data model
  • Repositories, environments, CI/CD, analytics
  • Initial Figma review and component mapping
  • Authentication and account foundation
2Weeks 3-6

Core Features

  • Artist profiles and work publishing
  • Image, video, and audio upload flows
  • Versions, critique requests, and responses
  • Discovery feed, filters, replies, and threads
3Weeks 7-10

Community & Communication

  • 1:1 messaging and push notifications
  • Consent, privacy, reporting, and blocking
  • Content visibility and moderation workflows
  • End-to-end community experience demo
4Weeks 11-14

Operations & Beta

  • Admin dashboard and user management
  • Web billing, subscriptions, and entitlements

Production Launch & Handover

  • App Store and Play Store submissions
  • Production web and backend deployment

  • Source code, documentation, and team handover

Two Product Deliverables

The product will ship in two deliberate stages. The August beta validates the critique experience, while the December release delivers the complete commercial platform.

August 1, 2026

Beta Build: Core Product

A working mobile-first beta centered on the complete critique experience.

  • Onboarding, profiles, and multi-format work uploads
  • Request, give, read, and reply to structured critiques
  • Discovery, direct messaging, and notifications
  • Consent, reporting, moderation, and thin admin
December 2026

Full Product: Feature Complete

The complete Greenchip platform, ready for public launch and monetization.

  • Free, Pro, Supporter, and Professional account types
  • Three-view profiles, vanity URLs, and private analytics
  • Patronage, marketplace, editions, and web payments
  • Events, classifieds, collections, and institution tooling
